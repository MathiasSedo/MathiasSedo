Mathias Sedo is a photographer and visual artist based in Aarhus, Denmark. His practice moves between experimental film negatives, carousel projection installations, and documentary photography — working with light, material, and the chemical residue of images left to decay.

Aarhus, Denmark
Work
Carousel projection — live installation

Light through physical material.
Not a screen. Not a laptop.

Available for hire with carousel projection for events, concerts, exhibitions, and private occasions. Live, organic, and unrepeatable — light pressed through experimental film negatives, transforming under the heat of the projector.

Every image differs from the last. No loops, no files. A projector, physical material, and the space you have.
