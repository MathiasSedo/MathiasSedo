Mathias Sedó works with materials that let light shine through them. Experimenting and drawing inspiration from natural objects, remnants of photographic history, and analogue colour universes, he explores what becomes visible when light finds its way through, and how the objects it illuminates can appear entirely different depending on the surface it lands on and the light they are seen by — all in pursuit of painting with light.
